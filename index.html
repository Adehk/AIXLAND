AIXLAND REVOLUTION

Aixland - The first global industrial Metaverse, representing an innovative solution for coordinating and managing all types of real estate and infrastructure, taking related operational and business processes to a new level of efficiency and interconnectedness.

Features and Functions

  • Virtual tours of real estate

    The ability to navigate through photorealistic 3D models of real estate. Interactive elements that allow users to change lighting conditions, furniture, or even the configuration of a room in real time.

  • Interaction with digital counterparts

    The ability to interact with digital counterparts to obtain data or simulate scenarios.

  • Configuration and personalization

    The ability to customize the virtual space for yourself, with visualization of various scenarios.

  • Transactional processes

    Blockchain databases and conducting smart contracts in transactions, with various options for conducting transactions, including cryptocurrency.

  • Communication tools

    Various tools for effective communication in the metaverse between users.

  • Data analysis and reporting

    The use of artificial intelligence for data analysis and simulation in a virtual environment.

  • Integration of virtual reality

    Full immersion with VR.

  • A multi-user experience.

    The possibility of collaboration and interaction within the metaverse.

Benefits for Users

The metaverse as a digital interface is designed to combine the physical world of real estate and infrastructure with the flexibility and unlimited possibilities of digital space.

It will completely transform the way real estate stakeholders, such as owners, tenants, investors and managers, do business. The platform will remove the spatial and temporal barriers associated with real estate management and will allow for accurate monitoring and analysis of operational processes in real time.
